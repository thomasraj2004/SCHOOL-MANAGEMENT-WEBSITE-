About Us

St. Britto Higher Secondary School was founded by the then Archbishop of Madurai Arch Diocese, Most Rev. Dr. Justin Diraviam, on 22nd June 1968. Our mission is to provide academic excellence combined with moral and spiritual values.

The school is named after St. John De Britto, who devoted his life to faith, justice, and service. For more than five decades, St. Britto has nurtured students to become leaders with integrity and compassion.

We believe in holistic education — empowering students intellectually, emotionally, and socially to contribute positively to society.

High School

Our High School provides a robust curriculum focusing on academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal growth.

Laboratories

Our laboratories are fully equipped to provide hands-on practical knowledge in science and technology.

We have separate labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science, enabling students to explore under expert supervision.

Library

Our library offers a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources. It encourages reading habits and supports academic excellence.

Students enjoy a peaceful reading environment for learning and research.
